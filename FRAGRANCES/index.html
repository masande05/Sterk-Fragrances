About Us

STERK FRAGRANCES is a South African-based perfume retailer passionate about delivering luxurious scents at accessible prices. Founded to bring carefully curated Arabic, designer-inspired and niche fragrances to our community, we blend quality, authenticity, and exceptional customer service.

We source perfumes from trusted suppliers, perform quality checks, and offer fast local delivery. Whether you're searching for oud, musk, woody, or fresh florals, our collection is selected to cater to every taste — Men, Women and Unisex.

Our promise: genuine products, transparent pricing and friendly service. Want to bulk order or need a gift recommendation? Chat with us on WhatsApp — we’ll help you pick the perfect scent.

Delivery Info

Delivery within major South African cities typically takes 1–3 business days. Rural deliveries can take longer. Standard delivery costs and expedited options are available at checkout.

We provide tracking numbers for all parcels. Please ensure your address and phone number are correct when placing orders.

Returns Policy

If you're not happy with an unopened product, contact us within 7 days of delivery to arrange a return. Opened or used perfumes cannot be accepted unless faulty. Faulty items will be replaced or refunded upon inspection.
